# Privacy Policy

Tello ("we", "us") is operated by **Tello Ltd**, a company registered in England and Wales. You can contact us at [privacy@zach13.com](mailto:privacy@zach13.com).

---

## 2. What data we collect

When you join the Tello early access waitlist, we collect:

- Your **first name**
- Your **email address**

We do not collect any other personal data through this page.

---

## 3. Why we collect it and our lawful basis

We collect your name and email to:

- Add you to the Tello early access waitlist — **lawful basis: legitimate interests**
- Send you updates about Tello's launch and early access availability — **lawful basis: consent** (by submitting the form, you agree to receive these emails)

## 5. How long we keep your data

We retain your name and email until:

- You request deletion (see Section 7), or
- Tello's early access programme ends and the data is no longer needed

---

## 6. Cookies

We use cookies for website analytics (Google Analytics). These are **non-essential cookies** and are only set after you give consent via our cookie banner.

If you decline cookies, only strictly necessary cookies will be used and Google Analytics will not be loaded.

You can change your cookie preference at any time using the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

---

## 7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Object** to processing
- **Restrict** processing
- **Data portability**

To exercise any of these rights, email us at **privacy@zach13.com**. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the **Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)**: [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk)
